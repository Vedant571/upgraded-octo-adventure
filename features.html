Features of a Prime Number


  1. Definition:-

    • A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that has exactly two factors: 1 and itself.

  2. Smallest Prime:-

    • The smallest prime number is 2, and it is the only even prime number.

  3. Odd Primes:-

    • All prime numbers except 2 are odd.

  4. Infinite:-

    • There are infinitely many prime numbers.

  5. Fundamental in Mathematics:-

    • rime numbers are the building blocks of all natural numbers (Prime Factorization).
